Your Information

When you sign in to our site, we get a bunch of information from Discord's OAuth provider. Additionally, when you submit content to our site, we also get a bunch of data from you. The following table outlines what we collect, when we collect it and why we collect it.

what when why
Discord ID, username, discriminator, avatar hash, whether you're a bot, have your account verified, and whether you have 2FA enabled Initial login with Discord Identification on the site for other members, displaying your avatar, uniquely identifying you when necessary
Guild ID, name, icon hash, permissions, and whether you're the owner on the guilds you're a member of Initial login with Discord Checking whether you're a member of the guild, determining your permissions on the website
Content submitted by you When you submit content To make use of your content, we actually need to store it somewhere.

Of course, you are able to delete your account at any time and for any reason if you want to. This will also delete any content generated by you on the site that isn't strictly necessary for proper operation of the website or retained for moderation reasons.
